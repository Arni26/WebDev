Fluffy Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2–3
  • Level: Easy
Ingredients Quantity
Flour1 cup
Milk3/4 cup
Egg1
Sugar1 tbsp
Baking powder2 tsp
SaltPinch
Butter (melted)2 tbsp

Steps:

  1. Mix dry ingredients (flour, sugar, baking powder, salt).
  2. Whisk milk, egg, and melted butter in a second bowl.
  3. Combine wet + dry (don’t overmix; small lumps are ok).
  4. Cook on a warm pan until bubbles form, then flip and finish.

Simple Guacamole

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 2–4
  • Level: Very easy
Ingredients Quantity
Ripe avocados2
Lime juice1–2 tbsp
SaltTo taste
Tomato (diced)1 small
Onion (finely chopped)2 tbsp
Black pepperOptional

Steps:

Step 1: Mash the avocados in a bowl.

Step 2: Mix in lime juice, salt, tomato, and onion.

Step 3: Taste and adjust (more lime or salt if needed).

Source: Wikipedia (Guacamole)
